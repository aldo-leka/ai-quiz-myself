# QuizPlus UGC Creator Brief

Use this brief for Billo, TikTok One, or direct outreach to freelance creators.

## Product

- Brand: `QuizPlus`
- URL: `https://quizplus.app`
- Core promise: Turn a topic, article, or PDF into a playable trivia game in minutes.
- Main modes:
  - `Single Player`
  - `Couch Co-op`
  - `WWTBAM` / millionaire-style mode

## Campaign Goal

Create short vertical videos that make people think:

- `I could use this tonight`
- `This looks easy to make`
- `This feels more fun than a normal quiz builder`

Primary CTA:

- `Try it from the link in bio`
- `Make one for your group`
- `Upload your notes and play tonight`

## What We Are Selling

We are not selling an AI tool in the abstract.

We are selling these use cases:

- Birthday trivia from shared notes or a PDF
- Movie trivia night from a topic or article
- Study or review quizzes from a PDF
- A custom millionaire-style quiz round for a party or stream

## Target Viewer

- 18-34
- Likes trivia, party games, study hacks, or fandom content
- Might host game night, birthday events, movie nights, or casual hangouts
- Wants something fast and easy, not a complicated setup

## Creator Angle

The video should feel like:

- `I found a fun tool and tested it`
- `Here is the result`
- `This is why it is useful`

It should not feel like:

- a fake testimonial
- a polished corporate ad
- an overexplained software tutorial

## Non-Negotiable Product Truths

Keep these accurate:

- QuizPlus can generate quizzes from:
  - a topic
  - a URL/article
  - a PDF
- Personal/private trivia works best when the source material is actually provided, especially via PDF.
- QuizPlus has different play modes, including couch co-op and millionaire-style gameplay.
- The product should be shown as fast and playable, not as a complex dashboard workflow.

Do not imply:

- the model magically knows private stories without source material
- real multiplayer voice chat exists if it does not
- rewards, prizes, or guarantees that do not exist

## Required Video Specs

- Vertical `9:16`
- Length: `15-25 seconds`
- Fast hook in first `1-2 seconds`
- Captions burned into video
- Must show product UI at least briefly
- Must show the gameplay/result, not just the creation flow

## Creative Structure

Recommended structure:

1. Hook
2. Quick proof of setup
3. Gameplay or reaction payoff
4. CTA

## Hook Examples

- `We turned a PDF into tonight's trivia game.`
- `This makes a custom trivia night in under a minute.`
- `I made a Who Wants to Be a Millionaire round from one topic.`
- `This is a way better party trick than generic trivia apps.`
- `I uploaded my notes and got a playable quiz out of it.`

## Example Angles

### Angle 1: Birthday PDF

- Hook: `We made a birthday quiz from shared notes and screenshots.`
- Show:
  - PDF upload
  - quick game creation
  - couch co-op gameplay
- CTA: `Link in bio if you want to make one for your group.`

### Angle 2: Movie Night

- Hook: `Movie trivia night in 30 seconds.`
- Show:
  - topic or URL input
  - couch co-op or solo round
  - someone getting a question wrong/right
- CTA: `Make your own movie round.`

### Angle 3: Millionaire Mode

- Hook: `This makes a custom millionaire-style game.`
- Show:
  - mode selection
  - dramatic gameplay
  - final answer / host audio / tension
- CTA: `Try it from the link in bio.`

## Visual Direction

- Strong close-up framing
- Use hands, face, phone, laptop, TV, or desk setup if possible
- Quick cuts over slow screen recordings
- Make the result feel immediate and social

## Tone

- Curious
- Direct
- Slightly impressed
- Casual, not overly salesy

## Deliverables Request

Ask the creator for:

- `3` videos minimum
- `2` hook variations per concept if budget allows
- raw footage if available
- edited version with captions
- usage rights for organic posting and paid usage if budget allows

## What To Avoid

- Fake claims
- Long intros
- Low-energy narration
- Showing only menus/forms for the whole video
- Saying `AI-powered` repeatedly without explaining the use case
- Generic talking-head praise with no proof

## CTA Links

Use campaign-specific links when posting. Examples:

- `https://quizplus.app/birthday-trivia-game?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=birthday_pdf&utm_content=creator1`
- `https://quizplus.app/movie-trivia-night?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=movie_night&utm_content=creator1`
- `https://quizplus.app/millionaire-game-online?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=millionaire_mode&utm_content=creator1`

## Approval Criteria

Approve videos that:

- hook fast
- show the product quickly
- show the payoff clearly
- stay accurate
- make the viewer understand the use case in under 10 seconds

Reject videos that:

- feel fake
- are too generic
- overfocus on the tool instead of the outcome
- hide the gameplay moment
